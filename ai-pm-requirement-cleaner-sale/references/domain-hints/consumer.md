# Domain Pack: toC / Consumer Products

## Applicable Keywords

Growth, retention, conversion, funnel, DAU, activation, push, sharing, A/B testing, cold start.

---

## Diagnostic Enhancement

Auxiliary dimensions (not included in total score):
1. `C1 User Mind Clarity`: whether the target user, motivation, and abandonment points are clearly defined
2. `C2 Growth Loop Completeness`: whether acquisition -> activation -> retention -> recall forms a closed loop

---

## Additional Follow-up Questions (select based on blocking items)

1. Is the goal acquisition, activation, or retention? What is the primary metric?
2. At which step do users most easily abandon? What was the most recent churn scenario?
3. Do incentive mechanisms (points/rewards) conflict with the existing system?
4. What is the push/notification strategy? How is disruption that leads to uninstalls avoided?
5. During cold start without content or data, how does the first screen avoid being empty?

---

## PRD Extension Suggestions

Prioritize using:
1. Core universal template (clearly define business goals and evidence)
2. Overlay Extension Pack B (rules/policy category) as needed to handle rewards and trigger rules
3. Only overlay Extension Pack A when system interaction complexity warrants it

- First-time experience and empty states
- Weak network and failure fallback
- Event tracking definitions and A/B metrics
